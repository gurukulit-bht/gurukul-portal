🎮 Course Mechanics (Apply to ALL Courses)

Each course must follow the same structure:

Per Chapter:
Short, simple story
Key lesson/value
Visual/icon
Quiz (3–5 MCQs)
Quiz Rules:
Instant feedback:
✅ Correct → “Awesome!”
❌ Incorrect → “Try again!”
Minimum passing score: 70%
Unlock next chapter only after passing
📊 Progress Tracking
Show:
Progress bar (e.g., 3/6 completed)

Track course completion status
