Create a bright, playful, and highly interactive “Student’s Corner” for the Bhartiya Hindu Temple Gurukul website. This section is designed for kids (ages 5–14) and should feel like a fun learning playground that teaches Hindu values through stories, quizzes, and rewards.

🌈 Design & Experience (VERY IMPORTANT)
Make the UI extremely kid-friendly
Use:
Bright colors (yellow, saffron, sky blue, green)
Cartoon-style visuals (Ram, Krishna, Hanuman, Ganesha as friendly characters)
Rounded buttons, big icons, playful fonts
Add:
Animations (confetti, stars, sparkles)
Fun sound effects (optional)
Keep navigation simple and intuitive
Avoid heavy text — use short sentences + visuals
